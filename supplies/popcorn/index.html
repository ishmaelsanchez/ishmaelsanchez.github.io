Glaze Pop

This is a Frosted Popcorn Mix that allows you to add different flavors to your popcorn without using a separate coating machine. You can coat your popcorn with this molten sugar glaze directly in the kettle as the popcorn pops. Glaze Pop contains a special ingredient which bonds sugar and oil together to guard against burning.
