Frozen Drinks / Frusheez™ Mixes

Frozen or chilled, Frusheez™ frozen drink mix is great for making drinks fast. To make a 2-1/2 gallon batch, mix together 1/2 gallon of mix plus 2 gallons of water.

Note when making alcholic drinks production is faster when you put the liquor in the glass and then add the frozen drink to it otherwise the liquor makes it difficult to freeze to maximum capacity.
