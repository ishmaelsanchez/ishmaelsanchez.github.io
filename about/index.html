About The Popcorn Company

We are a wholesale and retail distributor of popcorn, popcorn machines and all concession equipment and supplies. The Popcorn Company LLC is family owned and has been in business over 20 years. We have been a long time distributor for Weaver Popcorn, BKH Magic Mushroom Popcorn, Gold Medal Products, and Cretors, machines and supplies.

Our company is unique in that we provide free popcorn machines to businesses with the purchase of pre-portion popcorn kits. Our company prides itself on courteous and friendly employee's and impeccable service.
